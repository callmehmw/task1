Welcome to Your HMW COTTAGE INDISTRIES

HMW Cottage Industries, a subsidiary of Sri Sai Cottage Industries, embodies the essence of Indian craftsmanship. It’s a hub where traditional skills meet modern design, creating unique products that celebrate India’s artisanal heritage. This enterprise not only supports local artisans but also contributes to preserving the cultural tapestry of India’s cottage industry. HMW Cottage Industries is a shining example of sustainable business practices, fostering community growth and self-reliance.
